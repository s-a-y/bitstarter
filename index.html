For offline business

Do you want to greet customer by his name when he calls you first time? This is your opportunity! @Identity is made to share customer details between companies without giving access to client database. It allows you to identify customer when he calls you, to access his address or any other information immediately if request is approved by customer.

FAQ

How it works?
Pretty much the same way as OpenID does, there are providers, ids and users. For authorization and authentication something similar to OAuth used, but for offline commerce customer receives auth requests to the mobile phone. More on that we are building scalable and secure auth software which may be used by companies to get, organize and share client information.
How much it costs?
After we create initial infrastructure it's gonna be open source. It's free for customers and free to use until your business requires own installation. However we need to support our developers, that's what this crowdfunding website is about.
