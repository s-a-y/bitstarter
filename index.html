Increase your data-connection speed!

What data-connection speed your network carrier promises you? Maybe 1Mbit? 10Mbit? 100Mbit?
How much you actually get? 10-times less? 100-times less?
Now you can increase your speed using devices around you. Almost every modern device has WiFi or Bluetooth module, which can be used to transfer files much faster among people who's close to you, which means our devices can help each other to download data from internet.

Call for free to those who's reachable by p2p

If you're in the small town or in buildings across the road you don't actually need GSM network infrastructure to call to your friend, just use direct connection over GPRS/CDMA/UMTS or over Wi-Fi through other nodes in the network.
